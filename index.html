Insider & Congress Trade Monitor

Informational only — not investment advice. Everything here is compiled from mandatory public disclosures (SEC EDGAR Form 4 & 13F, congressional STOCK Act filings). Congressional amounts are reported in ranges, so dollar figures for politicians are midpoint estimates. The score is heuristic signal strength based on published research — it is not a probability of profit. Disclosures lag reality: insiders ~2 business days, Congress 30–45 days, funds up to 45 days after quarter-end.
Sources: SEC EDGAR (sec.gov — Form 4 daily indexes, 13F info tables, company facts) · Senate/House Stock Watcher community mirrors of official STOCK Act filings · unitedstates.io congressional photos (public domain). All sources are free and keyless. This tool runs entirely on your computer; no accounts, no tracking. Verify anything before acting on it — filings occasionally contain errors and community mirrors can lag.
